Test page: Language names, large sample

Observations 2025-01-07: Of the combos tested today, only JAWS announces language names. "Known" examples are e.g. English, Spanish, German; while Lower Serbian (dsb) is not known. Which languages are "known" varies by screen reader, that's part of what we're exploring here.

Windows 10, JAWS, Chrome:

  • Known language, with voice available: Announces digits in this language. Example: "veinte" for Spanish (es).
  • Known language, without voice available: Announces the language name, then the digits in English. Example: "Croatian, twenty-seven" (hr).
  • Unknown language: Announces digits in English (the language of the page).

Android TalkBack + Chrome:

  • Known language: Announces digits in this language.
  • Unknown language: Announces digits in the most recent language touched.

Android TalkBack + Chrome:

  • Announces all digits in English (the language of the page), for both known and unknown languages.
